On Par Connections is a non-profit organization that strives to connect like-minded students and professionals. As an organization we succeed in huge part due to our donors and event sponsors. Our expenses include but are not limited to; food and drink costs for events, travel expenses for group field trips, and promotional expenses for both student and professional recruitment. Any donation of $1,000 or more will automatically result in an event sponsorship unless you request otherwise. We thank you in advance for your contribution to our growing organization.

Donations are tax deductible as On Par Connections is a legal 501(c)(3)
